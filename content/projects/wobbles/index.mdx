<h2>Game Summary</h2>

Wobbles is a 2d puzzle platformer in the style of Lemmings built in Unity. Wobbles won the student division of the 2013 MassDiGI Game Challenge and was shipped on Android and iOS in August 2013.
Achievements:

<div style="text-align: left">

- Winner of best student entertainment game at the 2013 MassDiGI Game Challenge.
- Wobbles was accepted and showed at the Boston Festival of Indie Games
- As of May 2014: Averaged a combined rating of 4 out of 5 from Android and iOS store reviews.
- As of May 2014: 21,000 unique users since launch with an average of 500 monthly active users.

</div>

<h2>My Part</h2>

<div style="text-align: left">

After Wobbles won best student entertainment game at the 2013 MassDiGI game challenge. Our team was invited to the
MassDiGI summer innovation program. An Incubator where we were given the space to finish development and release Wobbles
on Apple App Store and Google Play.

- Worked on a five person team to build and publish an original game called Wobbles using Unity.
- Responsible for the creation of the sixty puzzle based levels that shipped with the game.
- Managed QA testing sessions while analyzing and documenting testers feedback.
- Iterate on our core design based on user testing and analytics.

</div>
